# resistance

## Plain-English meaning
Resistance is an area where price has tended to run into selling pressure or difficulty moving higher.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, resistance refers to a zone where price repeatedly struggles to establish itself above. Like support, it is usually better understood as an area than a perfect line.

## Why it matters
Resistance helps users understand why breakouts are interesting and why failed holds matter.
