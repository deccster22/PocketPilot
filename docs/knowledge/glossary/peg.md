# peg

## Plain-English meaning
A peg is a target value an asset is supposed to stay close to.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, peg usually refers to a stablecoin or similar asset intended to hold a fixed relationship to something else, often the U.S. dollar. The asset may wobble around the peg without fully breaking it.

## Why it matters
Understanding the peg helps users understand what a depeg really means. Not every wobble is a system failure, but not every wobble is harmless either.
