# slippage

## Plain-English meaning
Slippage is when the price you actually get is worse than the price you expected.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, slippage refers to execution quality deteriorating because of poor liquidity, fast movement, thin books, or fragmented conditions.

## Why it matters
A setup can look fine on paper and still perform badly in real action if slippage is severe.
