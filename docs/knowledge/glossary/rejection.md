# rejection

## Plain-English meaning
Rejection means the market tried to move into an area but failed to stay there.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, rejection refers to price being pushed away from a level or zone rather than building durable acceptance around it. A rejection can happen at support, resistance, or inside a broader structure.

## Why it matters
Rejection helps users see that “price touched the level” is not the same thing as “price established itself there.” It is especially important in breakout and range examples.
