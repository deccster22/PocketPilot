# Momentum

## What this means
The strength and speed of price movement.

## Why it matters
It helps explain whether a move is gaining energy or fading.

## How to think about it
Use momentum as a behaviour clue alongside structure and context.

## Common misunderstanding
Assuming strong momentum cannot reverse quickly.
