# What Dashboard Is For

## What this is
Dashboard is PocketPilot’s deeper working surface. It is where the product can show more context, structure, and reasoning behind the current read.

## Why it matters
A market can look simple on the surface while still being messy underneath. Dashboard is where things like [meaningful change](../glossary/meaningful-change.md) and [strategy fit](../glossary/strategy-fit.md) can be understood with more depth.

## What to pay attention to
If Snapshot is the fast read, Dashboard is the focus surface.
