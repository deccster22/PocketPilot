# ASSET_NARRATIVE_MODEL.md

## Purpose
Defines how PocketPilot may tell the story of an asset over time without drifting into mythology or prediction.

## Founding stance
PocketPilot may explain an asset’s recent story.
It may not turn that story into fate.

## Product job
Asset narrative should:
- summarize event-grounded behavior
- remain strategy-shaped
- remain account-aware where needed
- help users understand pattern and change over time

## Non-negotiables
- no anthropomorphism
- no prophecy
- no hidden recommendation
- no smoothing messy truth into prettier fiction

## Narrative posture
Good narrative is:
- measured
- specific
- event-aware
- temporally grounded
- calm

Bad narrative is:
- dramatic
- certain
- mystical
- pushy
