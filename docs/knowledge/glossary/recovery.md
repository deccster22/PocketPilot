# recovery

## Plain-English meaning
Recovery means the market has rebuilt more than just a short bounce.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, recovery refers to a stronger rebuild in structure, confidence, or operating conditions than a simple rebound or relief move. Recovery suggests some meaningful repair, not just motion away from the lows.

## Why it matters
Many users blur rebound and recovery together. PocketPilot separates them because a market can bounce without yet showing durable repair.
