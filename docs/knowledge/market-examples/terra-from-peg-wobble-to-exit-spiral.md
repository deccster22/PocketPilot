# Example: Terra From Peg Wobble to Exit Spiral

## Context
Terra unfolded inside an already fragile risk-off environment, where liquidity quality and trust were deteriorating before the full collapse became obvious.

## Setup
A strategy user could see early peg wobble, stabilization attempts, and rising stress signals across liquidity, reserves, and venue conditions. The key interpretation question was whether this was a temporary dislocation or a deeper structural break.

## What happened
UST moved away from peg and failed to normalize cleanly. Stress escalated into heavy dilution, worsening execution conditions, contagion signals, and eventual chain halt behavior.

## What to take from it
- Volatility can represent structural breakdown, not only faster movement.
- Stacked warning signals can point to danger confluence rather than a cleaner setup.
- This example is about deterioration behavior and context shift, not about classifying a trade as good or bad.
