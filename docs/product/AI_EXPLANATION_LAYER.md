# AI_EXPLANATION_LAYER.md

## Purpose
Defines what AI explanation may do inside PocketPilot and what it must never be allowed to do.

## Founding stance
AI may clarify.
It may not invent truth, authority, or instructions.

## Allowed role
AI may:
- translate
- summarize
- connect
- compress
- support learning
- personalize explanation density within profile rules

## Forbidden role
AI may not:
- invent events
- upgrade estimated into confirmed
- predict as fact
- pressure action
- overrule canonical system truth
- add hype, urgency, or fake certainty

## Product rule
If AI disappeared tomorrow, the product should still make sense.
If not, AI is covering for the wrong problem.

## Best homes
- Insights
- Knowledge Layer
- selected Dashboard explanations

## Weak homes
- Snapshot
- execution-adjacent persuasion
