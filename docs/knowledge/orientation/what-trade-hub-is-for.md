# What Trade Hub Is For

## What this is
Trade Hub is PocketPilot’s action-support surface. It is where the product helps you think more carefully about acting.

## Why it matters
Markets can create urgency theatre easily. Trade Hub exists to reduce friction when action is appropriate, while still respecting context and risk.

## What to pay attention to
Trade Hub should feel supportive, not bossy.
