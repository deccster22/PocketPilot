# confluence

## Plain-English meaning
Confluence means different kinds of evidence point to the same area or idea.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, confluence is layered agreement across independent evidence families such as structure, market behavior, holder positioning, regime context, or supporting signals. It is not just repeating the same signal in several forms.

## Why it matters
Confluence can make an area more relevant without making it certain. PocketPilot uses confluence to teach why agreement matters, but also why agreement can still fail, degrade, or need confirmation.
