# When Execution Risk Changes the Read

## What this means
[Execution risk](../glossary/execution-risk.md) is the risk that even if your market read is directionally right, actually getting in or out may go badly.

## Why it matters
Charts can make conditions look cleaner than they are.

## How PocketPilot uses it
PocketPilot uses execution risk to keep price-only interpretation honest.

## Common mistake
A common mistake is acting as if being right about direction guarantees a usable outcome.
