# regime

## Plain-English meaning
Regime means the broader kind of market environment you are in.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, regime refers to the larger operating backdrop of the market, such as supportive continuation, stressed volatility, range-bound conditions, or broader deleveraging pressure. It is the bigger environment around the immediate chart.

## Why it matters
The same pattern can mean different things in different regimes. PocketPilot uses regime to help users see why a bounce, breakout, or dip may feel very different when the broader environment changes.
