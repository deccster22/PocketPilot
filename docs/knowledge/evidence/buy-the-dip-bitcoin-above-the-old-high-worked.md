# Example: Buy the Dip Continuation - Bitcoin Above the Old High

## Context
The market had already shifted above a major historical boundary, with continuation structure still broadly intact.

## Setup
A Buy the Dip user could see pullbacks developing inside an established up-structure and evaluate whether weakness looked temporary or structural.

## What happened
Pullbacks occurred, but the broader continuation behavior held. Price repeatedly recovered without quickly losing the larger structure.

## What to take from it
- Dip behavior is more useful when broader structure remains supportive.
- Cheapness alone is weak context if structure is breaking.
- The key risk is treating every pullback as equivalent regardless of regime or structure quality.
