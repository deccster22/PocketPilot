# noise

## Plain-English meaning
Noise is movement or information that looks active but does not change the read in a useful way.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, noise refers to price action, commentary, or signal activity that adds motion or intensity without adding enough real information to justify a different interpretation.

## Why it matters
PocketPilot needs this idea to stay calm and avoid reacting to every flicker.
