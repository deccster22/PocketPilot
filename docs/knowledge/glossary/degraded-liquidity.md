# degraded liquidity

## Plain-English meaning
Degraded liquidity means the market is still functioning, but much less cleanly than normal.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, degraded liquidity refers to weaker depth, worse fills, wider slippage, or reduced execution quality that does not yet amount to a full breakdown.

## Why it matters
This helps users understand the difference between a stressed market and a fully broken one.
