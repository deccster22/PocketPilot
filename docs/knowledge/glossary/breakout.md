# breakout

## Plain-English meaning
A breakout is when price moves out of an area that had been containing it.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, a breakout usually means price has moved beyond a meaningful boundary such as resistance, a range, or another clear structure level. A breakout print is not the same thing as a confirmed breakout hold.

## Why it matters
Users often mistake “price crossed the line” for “the market has truly changed.” PocketPilot treats breakouts as events that may still need confirmation through time, follow-through, and acceptance.
