# What Regime Means

## What this means
A [regime](../glossary/regime.md) is the broader kind of market environment you are in. It is the bigger backdrop around the immediate chart.

## Why it matters
The same pattern can mean something very different in a supportive environment than it does in a damaged or stressed one. Regime helps PocketPilot explain why a move can look attractive in one phase and fragile in another.

## What PocketPilot is trying to help you see
PocketPilot uses regime to zoom out. This helps stop the nearest breakout, bounce, or flush from pretending to be the whole truth.

## Common mistake
A common mistake is reading every setup in isolation.
