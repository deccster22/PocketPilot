# Range Trader

## What this strategy is for
Range Trader is for markets moving inside a [range](../glossary/range.md) rather than trending strongly.

## What it looks for
It cares about where price is inside the box and whether the market is being pushed away in [rejection](../glossary/rejection.md) rather than escaping into something more directional.

## What improves fit
Fit improves when the boundaries keep mattering and [volatility](../glossary/volatility.md) is active but not chaotic enough to make the box meaningless.

## What weakens fit
Fit weakens when the range is degrading, repeated tests are wearing the zone down, or a more durable escape is beginning.
