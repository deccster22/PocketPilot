# Urgency Theatre vs Real Signal

## What this means
Markets are very good at looking urgent. That does not mean they are giving good information.

## Why it matters
Fast candles, louder commentary, and big emotional swings can create false pressure to act.

## How PocketPilot uses it
PocketPilot should slow the user down where the market is noisy, emotionally loaded, or still too incomplete to deserve a strong read.

## Common mistake
A common mistake is treating intensity like quality.
