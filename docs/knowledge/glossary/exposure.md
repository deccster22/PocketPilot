# exposure

## Plain-English meaning
Exposure means how much of your money, attention, or risk is tied to a market move or setup.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, exposure refers to the amount of market risk a user is carrying through position size, overlap, or correlated ideas.

## Why it matters
A setup can be fine in isolation and still become risky if total exposure is already high.
