# Confirmation vs Invalidation

## What this means
[Confirmation](../glossary/confirmation.md) is when the market gives stronger evidence that a read is holding up. [Invalidation](../glossary/invalidation.md) is when the evidence moves far enough against that read that it no longer holds together.

## Why it matters
Markets often suggest an interpretation before they fully prove it. Then sometimes they keep proving it, and sometimes they break it.

## What PocketPilot is trying to help you see
PocketPilot should help users follow progression, not leap from zero to certainty.

## Common mistake
A common mistake is treating one good candle as full confirmation, or one messy candle as instant invalidation.
