# strategy fit

## Plain-English meaning
Strategy fit means how comfortable or suitable a strategy is for the current market environment.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, strategy fit is the relationship between a strategy’s assumptions and the current market conditions. A strategy can be a good strategy in general and still have poor fit in the current environment.

## Why it matters
This is one of PocketPilot’s most important teaching ideas. The same market can feel very different to different strategies, and the same strategy can feel very different in different phases.
