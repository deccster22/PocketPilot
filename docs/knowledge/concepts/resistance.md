# Resistance

## What this means
A price area where selling tends to appear.

## Why it matters
It helps explain why upward moves can pause, stall, or reverse.

## How to think about it
Treat resistance as a zone that can weaken over repeated tests.

## Common misunderstanding
Assuming resistance cannot be broken.
