# reserve backing

## Plain-English meaning
Reserve backing means the assets or support sitting behind something that is meant to stay stable.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, reserve backing refers to the reserves, collateral, or support assets intended to help maintain confidence in an asset such as a stablecoin or related system. Strong reserve backing and weak reserve backing do not create the same risk profile.

## Why it matters
This term helps users understand why two things that look similar on the surface can behave very differently under stress. PocketPilot uses it as a context term, not just a technical detail.
