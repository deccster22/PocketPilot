# Trend Follow

## What this strategy is for
Trend Follow is for situations where the market is already moving directionally and continuing to behave that way.

## What it looks for
It often becomes more relevant after a [breakout](../glossary/breakout.md), reclaim, or broader structural shift.

## What improves fit
[Strategy fit](../glossary/strategy-fit.md) improves when the broader [regime](../glossary/regime.md) is supportive and the market keeps proving the direction.

## What weakens fit
Fit weakens when the direction is fragile, the environment remains damaged, or the market looks more like temporary rebound than durable continuation.

## What PocketPilot would emphasize
Trend Follow is persistence-aware rather than prediction-obsessed.

## What a user should learn
You do not need the exact low for Trend Follow to matter.
