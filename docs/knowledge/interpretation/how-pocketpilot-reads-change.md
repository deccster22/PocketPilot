# How PocketPilot Reads Change

## What this means
PocketPilot does not try to react to every flicker. It tries to notice [meaningful change](../glossary/meaningful-change.md), which means change that actually affects interpretation.

## Why it matters
Markets move constantly. If the product treated all movement as equally important, it would become noisy and much less useful.

## How PocketPilot uses it
PocketPilot uses meaningful change to decide when the read deserves updating, when a strategy may feel different, and when the situation has shifted enough to warrant more attention.

## Common mistake
A common mistake is treating all motion as equally meaningful.
