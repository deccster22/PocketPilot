# deleveraging

## Plain-English meaning
Deleveraging means borrowed risk and excess exposure are being forced out of the market.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, deleveraging refers to a market phase where leverage, credit exposure, or overly stretched risk positions are being reduced, often through forced selling, liquidations, tighter conditions, or shrinking confidence.

## Why it matters
Deleveraging changes what feels comfortable. Strategies that worked well in easy, supportive conditions can become much more fragile when leverage is being unwound and liquidity is tightening.
