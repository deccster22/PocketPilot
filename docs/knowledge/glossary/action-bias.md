# action bias

## Plain-English meaning
Action bias is the urge to do something just because doing nothing feels uncomfortable.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, action bias refers to pressure toward activity that comes from psychology or discomfort rather than strong setup quality or market support.

## Why it matters
PocketPilot should reduce bad action loops, not feed them.
