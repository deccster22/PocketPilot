# Buy the Dip Caution Example: Bitcoin June to August 2022 Relief Rally

## What happened
After sharp downside stress, Bitcoin bounced strongly enough that buying weakness could easily feel attractive again.

## Why this looked tempting
The rebound created the impression that the worst damage might already be done, making “buying the dip” sound more reasonable than it actually was.

## What made the fit fragile
- the broader environment remained damaged
- rebound energy was easy to overread as repair
- cheapness looked clearer than structural safety
- the market had not yet earned enough stronger confirmation

## What later weakened or invalidated the case
Later weakness showed that the environment was still more fragile than the bounce implied.

## What a user could learn
A market being down a lot does not automatically mean the dip is the opportunity. Structure still matters.
