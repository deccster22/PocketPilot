# Buy the Dip Worked Example: Bitcoin Above the Old High

## What happened
Within a broader supportive market environment, pullbacks appeared without destroying the larger continuation structure.

## Why this fit the strategy
This is a useful Buy the Dip case because weakness happened inside a still-constructive environment rather than inside a market that was already coming apart.

## What supported the read
- the broader structure remained supportive
- dips looked like temporary weakness rather than structural damage
- continuation logic stayed intact
- the environment still gave repair room rather than immediately punishing weakness

## What PocketPilot would have emphasized
A dip is not automatically good. It becomes more interesting when the surrounding structure still looks usable.

## What a user could learn
Buy the Dip works best when “cheaper” and “still structurally supported” are happening together.
