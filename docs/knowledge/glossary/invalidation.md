# invalidation

## Plain-English meaning
Invalidation means the evidence has moved far enough against an earlier interpretation that the old read no longer holds up.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, invalidation is the point where an earlier thesis, setup, or confidence state is no longer well supported by the market. It is stronger than “less comfortable” and different from “not perfect.”

## Why it matters
Invalidation keeps PocketPilot honest. It stops the product from clinging to an old read after the market has changed in a more meaningful way.
