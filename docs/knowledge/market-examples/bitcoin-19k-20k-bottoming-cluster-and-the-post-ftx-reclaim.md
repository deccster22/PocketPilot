# Example: Bitcoin 19k-20k Bottoming Cluster and the Post-FTX Reclaim

## Context
During 2022 stress and into early 2023, Bitcoin kept interacting with the 19k-20k zone while market confidence shifted through repeated tests, breakdown, and later reclaim behavior.

## Setup
A strategy user could see multiple independent evidence families around the same area: prior cycle structure, cost-basis references, and changing trend behavior. The core interpretation challenge was whether this cluster was strengthening, degrading, or failing.

## What happened
Price tested and crossed the zone repeatedly through mid and late 2022, which kept the area relevant but unresolved. The November FTX shock broke the zone materially. Later, January 2023 reclaim behavior above key references made the recovery structure more coherent than earlier summer attempts.

## What to take from it
- Confluence can increase relevance without guaranteeing durability.
- Repeated interaction can reflect both importance and unresolved structure.
- This example is about how evidence alignment can degrade and rebuild over time, not about labeling a trade as right or wrong.
