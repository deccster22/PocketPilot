# When Less Is the Strategy

## What this means
Sometimes the strongest move is not a bigger move. It is restraint.

## Why it matters
If the market is low-quality, fragile, or drowning in incomplete information, “doing less” may be the most disciplined response. This is especially true when [estimated confidence](../glossary/estimated-confidence.md) is thin, [volatility](../glossary/volatility.md) is messy, or [contagion](../glossary/contagion.md) is spreading.

## How PocketPilot uses it
PocketPilot should not reward action bias. It should help users see when patience is not hesitation, but a cleaner response to weak conditions.

## Common mistake
A common mistake is treating inactivity like failure.
