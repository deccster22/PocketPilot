# Confluence

## What this means
Multiple signals pointing the same way.

## Why it matters
It can strengthen interpretation quality when evidence is independent.

## How to think about it
Prefer disciplined agreement across different evidence types over indicator stacking.

## Common misunderstanding
Assuming more signals always means a better trade.
