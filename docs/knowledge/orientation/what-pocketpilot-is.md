# What PocketPilot Is

## What this is
PocketPilot is a strategy-first crypto decision-support product. It is designed to help you understand what deserves attention without pretending certainty.

## Why it matters
PocketPilot is trying to make the market easier to read. That means the product cares about [meaningful change](../glossary/meaningful-change.md), context, and [strategy fit](../glossary/strategy-fit.md), not just motion.

## What to pay attention to
A calmer question sits underneath the product: what kind of environment am I in, and how should I understand it? That is where the [30,000 ft View](../glossary/30000-ft-view.md) starts to matter.
