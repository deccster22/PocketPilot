# Example: Trend Follow Caution - From Liquidity Tailwind to Deleveraging Grind

## Context
Continuation-friendly conditions from the prior expansion phase were fading as macro and crypto-native deleveraging pressure increased.

## Setup
A Trend Follow user could still see familiar continuation behavior early in the transition, but had to evaluate whether the background was still supporting that behavior.

## What happened
As stress accumulated, continuation became less reliable. Prior trend assumptions weakened as the environment became harsher and less cooperative.

## What to take from it
- Trend-follow fit depends on environment quality, not only on recent direction.
- Regime deterioration can make old continuation patterns less durable.
- A caution read here is about changing context, not declaring the strategy itself invalid.
