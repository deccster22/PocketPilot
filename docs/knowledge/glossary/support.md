# support

## Plain-English meaning
Support is an area where price has tended to find buying interest or stop falling for a while.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, support refers to a zone where price behavior has repeatedly shown resistance to moving lower. Support is usually better thought of as an area than a magical line.

## Why it matters
Support helps users understand why some drops slow down, bounce, or matter more than others.
