# contagion

## Plain-English meaning
Contagion means stress in one part of the market spreads into other parts.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, contagion refers to pressure or failure spreading across assets, firms, venues, strategies, or market segments rather than remaining isolated. It often changes the environment more broadly than the original trigger alone.

## Why it matters
Contagion helps explain why one event can become a market-wide trust or liquidity problem. It is a key term for Market Examples about regime change, stablecoin stress, and credit damage.
