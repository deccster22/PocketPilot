# Why Strategy Fit Matters

## What this means
[Strategy fit](../glossary/strategy-fit.md) is the relationship between a strategy’s assumptions and the current market environment.

## Why it matters
The same market can feel comfortable for one strategy and awkward for another. The same strategy can also feel different in a different [regime](../glossary/regime.md).

## How PocketPilot uses it
PocketPilot uses strategy fit to explain why one lens may be improving while another stays fragile.

## Common mistake
A common mistake is assuming a good strategy is always a good fit right now.
