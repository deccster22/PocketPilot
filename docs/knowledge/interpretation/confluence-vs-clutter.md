# Confluence vs Clutter

## What this means
[Confluence](../glossary/confluence.md) means different kinds of evidence are pointing to the same area or idea. Clutter is when a page or setup looks busy, but much of that “extra evidence” is not actually independent.

## Why it matters
More inputs do not automatically mean a better read. PocketPilot wants users to see when evidence is layered and meaningful, and when it is just the same story dressed in extra costumes.

## What PocketPilot is trying to help you see
PocketPilot should reward independent agreement, not noise.

## Common mistake
A common mistake is assuming that five indicators always beat one.
