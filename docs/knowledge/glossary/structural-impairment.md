# structural impairment

## Plain-English meaning
Structural impairment means the market is not just volatile. Parts of the system that support normal behavior are also breaking down.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, structural impairment refers to a condition where underlying market function has degraded enough to damage normal interpretation, liquidity, exits, venue reliability, or trust in the operating environment.

## Why it matters
PocketPilot uses this term to distinguish “fast and scary” from “the market is no longer functioning normally.” That difference matters a lot for strategy comfort and execution.
