# risk stacking

## Plain-English meaning
Risk stacking means piling several related risks on top of each other without treating them like the same underlying bet.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, risk stacking refers to increased vulnerability created when several positions, setups, or assumptions all depend on similar market outcomes.

## Why it matters
It helps explain why several “small” risks can still add up to one big fragile situation.
