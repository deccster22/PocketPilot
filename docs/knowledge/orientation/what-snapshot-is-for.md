# What Snapshot Is For

## What this is
Snapshot is PocketPilot’s quick re-entry surface. Its job is to help you understand what is going on right now without forcing you into a deep analysis session.

## Why it matters
Snapshot gives a calm first read. It should help you see what changed and whether anything looks more relevant at a glance.

## What to pay attention to
Snapshot is not the whole truth. It is the fast read. If the situation is more complex, the deeper read matters more.
