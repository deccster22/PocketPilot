# P8-I7 - Year in Review Foundation

## Why This Phase Happened Now

P8-I1 created the first thin Insights history shelf.
P8-I2 added calm last-viewed continuity.
P8-I3 added one deeper interpreted archive shelf for event history.
P8-I4 added one compare-period reflection seam.
P8-I5 added one monthly / quarterly summary foundation.
P8-I6 added one calm summary-archive shelf.

That made the next safe step clear:

- keep building on the same interpreted history spine
- add one calm annual debrief without turning reflection into performance theatre
- do it through one service-owned Year in Review seam rather than UI-side recap shaping

P8-I7 happens now because PocketPilot already has enough event-history and summary groundwork to support one honest annual debrief, but it is still too early for exports, journaling expansion, AI commentary, or a broader analytics/reporting suite.

## What P8-I7 Added

P8-I7 adds five narrow refinements:

1. One canonical `YearInReviewVM` contract in `services/insights/types.ts`.
2. One service-owned annual debrief builder in `services/insights/createYearInReviewVM.ts`.
3. One canonical annual fetch seam in `services/insights/fetchYearInReviewVM.ts`.
4. One minimal subordinate `InsightsYearInReviewScreen` that renders prepared annual framing, debrief items, and limitations only.
5. One light handoff from `InsightsScreen` into a Year in Review path alongside summaries and summary archive.

The read path is now:

`EventLedger -> createYearInReviewVM -> fetchYearInReviewVM -> InsightsYearInReviewScreen`

## Reflection Rules Locked In Here

P8-I7 establishes these rules:

- one canonical Year in Review contract only
- annual period selection, debrief shaping, and honest availability stay in `services/`
- `app/` renders prepared title, summary, items, and limitations only
- low-change years are valid and should be stated honestly
- annual readbacks stay calm, contextual, descriptive, and non-moralizing
- raw ledger rows, provider/runtime metadata, scorecards, exports, journaling controls, and shame language stay out of Year in Review output

## What P8-I7 Deliberately Did Not Add

P8-I7 does not add:

- export flows
- journal editing
- freeform reflection writing
- rich compare-window customization
- scorecards
- streaks
- gamified analytics
- push notifications
- background polling
- AI-generated reflection commentary
- a social-share recap flow

## What Future P8 Work Can Build On

Later `P8` work can safely extend this seam with:

- richer annual archive handling once archive scope is explicitly widened
- journal attachment or reflection-note links once the log / journal family is ready
- additional calm compare-period tooling once it has its own scoped phase
- broader archive cross-links if the reflection family needs them later

Those later steps should deepen the current interpreted seam rather than replacing it with brag-reel recap flows, performance dashboards, or moralizing annual reviews.

## Recommendation Review

- Adopt now: One canonical Year in Review seam because P8-I5 and P8-I6 already established the calm summary/archive spine and the next honest step is one service-owned annual debrief.
- Add to backlog: Broader annual archive handling, journal linkage, and richer compare-period options because they may help later, but they widen the reflection family beyond this foundation.
- Decline for now: Exports, AI commentary, gamified annual recaps, and broader performance reporting because they would turn this phase into a different product mode.
