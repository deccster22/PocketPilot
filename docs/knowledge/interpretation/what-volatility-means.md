# What Volatility Means

## What this means
[Volatility](../glossary/volatility.md) means the market is moving around more sharply, quickly, or unpredictably than usual. In PocketPilot, volatility is not just about whether candles are big. It is about how unstable the environment feels and how much that changes interpretation.

## Why it matters
The same setup can feel much more comfortable in a calmer market than in a violent one. Volatility changes how much confidence deserves support, how quickly conditions can shift, and how much room different strategies may need.

## What PocketPilot is trying to help you see
PocketPilot treats volatility as context, not theatre. A volatile market is not automatically a good opportunity or a bad one. It is a market that deserves a different reading.

## Common mistake
A common mistake is treating volatility like excitement instead of information.
