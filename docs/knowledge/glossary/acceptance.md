# acceptance

## Plain-English meaning
Acceptance means the market is behaving like it is comfortable staying in a new area.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, acceptance refers to the market spending enough time, volume, and structure in a new zone that the move looks more durable than a brief spike. It is one of the clues that a breakout or reclaim may be becoming more trustworthy.

## Why it matters
A market can print above a level without being accepted there. PocketPilot uses acceptance to separate a move that merely happened from a move that the market is actually starting to live with.
