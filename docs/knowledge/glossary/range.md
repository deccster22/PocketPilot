# range

## Plain-English meaning
A range is a zone where price keeps moving back and forth rather than trending strongly in one direction.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, a range is a period or structure where price repeatedly interacts with an upper boundary and a lower boundary without clearly escaping either side for long.

## Why it matters
A range can make some strategies more comfortable and others less comfortable. It can also degrade over time if the market keeps testing the same boundaries without resolving cleanly.
