# Example: Buy the Dip Caution - Bitcoin June to August 2022 Relief Rally

## Context
Bitcoin was rebounding after deep downside stress, but broader damage from the prior decline remained unresolved.

## Setup
A Buy the Dip user could see sharp rebound behavior and infer that cheapness plus bounce energy might support recovery.

## What happened
The rebound was real, but later weakness showed that structural repair had not fully followed the relief move.

## What to take from it
- Down a lot does not automatically mean structurally repaired.
- Relief behavior can coexist with unresolved fragility.
- The key risk is over-weighting rebound speed while under-weighting broader damage.
