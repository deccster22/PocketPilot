# relief rally

## Plain-English meaning
A relief rally is a rebound that happens after heavy selling, when the pressure eases enough for price to bounce.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, a relief rally is a meaningful rebound after a stretched downside move that may improve short-term conditions without yet proving durable recovery.

## Why it matters
Relief rallies are easy to over-read. They can be real and still be fragile. PocketPilot uses the term to separate temporary improvement from stronger structural repair.
