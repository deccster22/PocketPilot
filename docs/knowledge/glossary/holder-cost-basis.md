# holder cost basis

## Plain-English meaning
Holder cost basis is an estimate of the prices where current holders got in.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, holder cost basis refers to the estimated acquisition prices of coins held by current market participants. Different holder groups can have different cost-basis measures, such as aggregate holders or long-term holders.

## Why it matters
Cost basis can help explain why some areas matter more than others. It gives PocketPilot another way to think about where pressure, comfort, or recovery may be building beneath the surface.
