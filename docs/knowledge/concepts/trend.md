# Trend

## What this means
Price moving consistently in one direction.

## Why it matters
It helps you decide whether market behaviour is persisting or shifting.

## How to think about it
Use trend as context for interpretation, not as a promise that direction must continue.

## Common misunderstanding
Thinking a trend guarantees future movement.
