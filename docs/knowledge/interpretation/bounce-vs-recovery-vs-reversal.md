# Bounce vs Recovery vs Reversal

## What this means
A bounce can be a short-term improvement after stress. A [recovery](../glossary/recovery.md) suggests more meaningful repair. A [reversal](../glossary/reversal.md) suggests a stronger directional change than a simple rebound.

## Why it matters
These words often get blurred together in live markets.

## How PocketPilot uses it
PocketPilot uses this distinction to stop temporary improvement from being described with stronger language than it deserves.

## Common mistake
A common mistake is calling every bounce a recovery, or every recovery a full reversal.
