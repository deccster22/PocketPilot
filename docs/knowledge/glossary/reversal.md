# reversal

## Plain-English meaning
A reversal is when the market changes direction in a more meaningful way than a brief bounce or pause.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, reversal refers to a directional shift that appears stronger and more durable than a simple counter-move. It usually needs more evidence than a short rebound to deserve confidence.

## Why it matters
Users often call every bounce a reversal. PocketPilot keeps the term more disciplined so the language stays useful and does not inflate confidence too early.
