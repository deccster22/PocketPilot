# overtrading

## Plain-English meaning
Overtrading means acting too often, too aggressively, or with too little quality control.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, overtrading refers to a pattern where action frequency outruns signal quality, setup quality, or environmental support.

## Why it matters
PocketPilot is designed to reduce bad action loops, not feed them.
