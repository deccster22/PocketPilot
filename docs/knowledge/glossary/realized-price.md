# realized price

## Plain-English meaning
Realized price is a way of estimating the average cost basis of coins currently held in the market.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, realized price refers to an on-chain cost-basis measure that estimates the aggregate price at which current holders acquired their coins. It is one way of understanding where broad holder positioning sits.

## Why it matters
Realized price can help show whether price is trading above or below broad holder cost basis. It becomes especially useful in deeper case studies about stress, bottoming, and recovery.
