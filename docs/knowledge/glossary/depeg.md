# depeg

## Plain-English meaning
A depeg happens when something that is supposed to stay near a fixed value moves away from it.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, depeg usually refers to a stablecoin or similar asset losing its intended price relationship, often to the U.S. dollar. A small wobble and a full depeg are not the same thing.

## Why it matters
A depeg can signal much more than price movement. It can point to weakness in reserves, liquidity, confidence, or market plumbing. PocketPilot uses it as a context term, not just a scary headline word.
