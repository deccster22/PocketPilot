# estimated confidence

## Plain-English meaning
Estimated confidence means the evidence is leaning in a direction, but the picture is not fully settled yet.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, estimated confidence is a bounded confidence state used when the current evidence suggests an interpretation is improving or plausible, but the market has not yet provided enough confirmation to treat it as established.

## Why it matters
This helps PocketPilot stay honest. Markets often give partial evidence before they give durable clarity. Estimated confidence lets the product acknowledge improvement without pretending certainty.
