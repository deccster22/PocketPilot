# signal quality

## Plain-English meaning
Signal quality means how trustworthy, relevant, and useful a signal looks in context.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, signal quality refers to the credibility of a signal once context, regime, support, and execution conditions are considered.

## Why it matters
A loud signal is not always a good signal.
