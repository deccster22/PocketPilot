# Choosing Your Experience Level and Strategy

## What this is
PocketPilot lets the same core system support different kinds of users. Your experience level changes how much help is surfaced, not the underlying truth.

## Why it matters
A Beginner may want stronger plain-English support, while an Advanced user may want less visual noise. Strategy choice matters because [strategy fit](../glossary/strategy-fit.md) changes what feels comfortable in the current market.

## What to pay attention to
The same chart can look different in a different [regime](../glossary/regime.md). Experience level changes support density, not reality.
