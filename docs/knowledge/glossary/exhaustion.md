# exhaustion

## Plain-English meaning
Exhaustion means a move may be getting so stretched that it starts losing force.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, exhaustion refers to a condition where price action, positioning, or selling/buying pressure looks stretched enough that the current move may be weakening or becoming easier to interrupt.

## Why it matters
Exhaustion can help explain why a bounce becomes possible without proving that a full recovery or reversal has arrived.
