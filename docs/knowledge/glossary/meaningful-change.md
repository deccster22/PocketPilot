# meaningful change

## Plain-English meaning
Meaningful change is a change that actually matters for interpretation, not just a small twitch in price or noise.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, meaningful change is a shift in market conditions, structure, fit, or evidence strong enough to affect how the market should be read. It is more than movement. It is movement that changes the interpretation.

## Why it matters
PocketPilot is built around explaining what matters, not reacting to every flicker. Meaningful change is one of the filters that helps the product stay calm and useful.
