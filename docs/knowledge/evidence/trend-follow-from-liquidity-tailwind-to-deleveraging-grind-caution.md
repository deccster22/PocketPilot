# Trend Follow Caution Example: From Liquidity Tailwind to Deleveraging Grind

## What happened
A supportive broader environment gave way to a harsher deleveraging phase, changing the conditions that had previously made continuation more comfortable.

## Why this looked tempting
Trend-follow logic can stay attractive when a market has already spent time behaving directionally and users become conditioned to continuation.

## What made the fit fragile
- broader regime quality deteriorated
- continuation assumptions became stale
- the environment changed faster than a simple trend lens could justify
- old persistence did not guarantee new persistence

## What later weakened or invalidated the case
As the macro backdrop shifted, continuation confidence deserved to weaken. The trend-follow read became less comfortable because the environment had changed, not because the strategy itself was “wrong.”

## What a user could learn
Trend following depends on the market continuing to cooperate. When the regime changes, old continuation logic can become much less trustworthy.
