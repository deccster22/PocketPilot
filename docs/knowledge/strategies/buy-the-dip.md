# Buy the Dip

## What this strategy is for
Buy the Dip is for situations where a [dip](../glossary/dip.md) may be creating opportunity without the broader picture being broken beyond repair.

## What it looks for
It looks for weakness happening inside an environment where [recovery](../glossary/recovery.md) still feels plausible.

## What improves fit
[Strategy fit](../glossary/strategy-fit.md) improves when the broader structure still looks supportive and the market is not obviously degrading into a harsher [regime](../glossary/regime.md).

## What weakens fit
Fit weakens when the move looks more like a [relief rally](../glossary/relief-rally.md) than repair, or when improvement is visible but still not enough to count as [meaningful change](../glossary/meaningful-change.md).
