# P5-R9 - Unsupported / Incomplete Risk Inputs Guidance

## Why This Phase Happened Now
P5-R6 through P5-R8 made the risk lane honest about the selected basis, the per-trade framing, the prepared sizing/max-loss output, and the preferred account basis. Once those pieces were explicit, the next honest step was to explain why the lane cannot yet finish when the prepared inputs are too thin or the selected basis is unsupported.

## What Was Added
- one canonical `RiskInputGuidance` contract and availability seam
- one service-owned guidance builder for thin or unsupported risk-input states
- one prepared Trade Hub preview path that can surface the calm guidance note
- one minimal screen-rendered guidance note in the existing Trade Hub lane

## What Was Not Added
- no live order dispatch
- no hard enforcement by default
- no app-side validation logic
- no troubleshooting maze
- no broker-specific support copy
- no noisy warning theatre

## What Future Work Can Build On
- richer support-first risk cues when more plan or account context is honestly available
- broader reuse of the same guidance seam in other prepared risk surfaces if needed
- deeper, still non-enforcing explanations for sizing and max-loss readiness without changing the current calm posture
