# What the Knowledge Library Is For

## What this is
The Knowledge Library is the part of PocketPilot that helps explain terms, strategies, case studies, and product concepts in a calmer, reusable way.

## Why it matters
A lot of market language that sounds obvious to experienced people is not obvious to everybody. The library exists so you can move from unfamiliar words to usable understanding without needing three separate versions of the truth.

## What to pay attention to
The library supports different levels of help. A Beginner may see stronger first-use support. An Advanced user may mostly use it on demand.
