# What Risk Means Inside PocketPilot

## What this means
Risk in PocketPilot is not just “could price go the wrong way.”

## Why it matters
A setup can look interesting while still carrying weak [strategy fit](../glossary/strategy-fit.md), poor [liquidity](../glossary/liquidity.md), or elevated [execution risk](../glossary/execution-risk.md).

## How PocketPilot uses it
PocketPilot should frame risk as a blend of market direction, environment quality, and action quality.

## Common mistake
A common mistake is treating risk as something that only exists when the chart looks ugly.
