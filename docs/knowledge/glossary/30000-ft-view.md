# 30,000 ft View

## Plain-English meaning
30,000 ft View means zooming out far enough to understand the broader environment instead of only staring at the nearest candle.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, 30,000 ft View is the high-altitude interpretation layer that helps users understand broad market phase, regime, and context. It is where the product steps back from the immediate chart to explain the larger setting.

## Why it matters
A local bounce, breakout, or flush can look very different once the broader environment is visible. PocketPilot uses 30,000 ft View to stop short-term price from pretending to be the whole truth.
