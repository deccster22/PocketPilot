# Example: From Liquidity Tailwind to Deleveraging Grind

## Context
Crypto moved from a liquidity-supportive expansion phase into a tightening and deleveraging phase where older continuation assumptions became less reliable.

## Setup
A strategy user could still see local rallies and familiar patterns early in the transition. The challenge was to decide whether those signals were still behaving in the same way under a changed macro backdrop.

## What happened
As 2022 progressed, pressure stacked across policy tightening and crypto-specific stress events. Conditions became less forgiving, and setups that worked in expansion became more fragile as deleveraging pressure continued.

## What to take from it
- Regime shifts often arrive in layers rather than one clean turning point.
- The same chart shape can behave differently when background conditions change.
- This example is about environment sensitivity and fit drift, not about outcome scoring.
