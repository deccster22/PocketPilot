# dip

## Plain-English meaning
A dip is a drop in price.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, a dip usually means a noticeable move lower inside an existing market sequence. A dip can happen inside a healthy trend, inside a weak market, or during a much bigger breakdown.

## Why it matters
Many users hear “buy the dip” as if every drop is automatically an opportunity. PocketPilot treats a dip as a description, not a verdict. The real question is what kind of environment the dip is happening inside.
