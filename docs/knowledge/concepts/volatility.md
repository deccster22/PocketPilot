# Volatility

## What this means
How much price moves over time.

## Why it matters
It affects risk framing, strategy comfort, and interpretation tone.

## How to think about it
Read volatility as market condition context, not as good or bad by itself.

## Common misunderstanding
Assuming high volatility always means opportunity.
