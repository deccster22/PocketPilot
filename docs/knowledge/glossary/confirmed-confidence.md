# confirmed confidence

## Plain-English meaning
Confirmed confidence means the evidence has become stronger, more durable, and less fragile than it was earlier.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, confirmed confidence is used when the market has provided stronger confirmation that an interpretation is holding up, rather than merely appearing possible. It still does not mean certainty or zero risk.

## Why it matters
Users often mistake confirmation for guarantee. PocketPilot uses confirmed confidence to describe a stronger read, not a perfect one.
