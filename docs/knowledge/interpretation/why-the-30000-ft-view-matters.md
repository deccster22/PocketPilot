# Why the 30,000 ft View Matters

## What this means
The [30,000 ft View](../glossary/30000-ft-view.md) means zooming out enough to understand the broader environment around the immediate chart.

## Why it matters
A local move can look very persuasive while still belonging to a much weaker backdrop.

## How PocketPilot uses it
PocketPilot uses this layer to explain context, [regime](../glossary/regime.md), and broader pressure.

## Common mistake
A common mistake is reading a market entirely through the nearest breakout, dip, or bounce.
