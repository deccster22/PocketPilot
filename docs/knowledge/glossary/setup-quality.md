# setup quality

## Plain-English meaning
Setup quality means how strong the overall case for action looks once the full context is considered.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, setup quality refers to the combined quality of the signal, the environment, the action conditions, and the downside if the read is wrong.

## Why it matters
It helps PocketPilot explain why some ideas deserve patience and others deserve restraint.
