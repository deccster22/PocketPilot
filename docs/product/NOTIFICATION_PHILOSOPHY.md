# NOTIFICATION_PHILOSOPHY.md

## Purpose
Defines what deserves interruption in PocketPilot and what does not.

## Founding stance
Notifications are part of the interpretation layer, not a retention weapon.

The guiding rule:
**notify when meaning changes, not when data twitches.**

## What notifications are for
- selective awareness
- calm return to context
- strategy discipline
- meaningful interpreted change

## What they are not for
- urgency
- theatre
- fear
- guilt
- compulsive checking
- raw market-noise feeds

## Channel posture
- passive state indicators first
- subtle in-app haptics second
- explicit in-app notices only when warranted
- push remains the most restricted and phase-sensitive channel

## Tone
PocketPilot notifications should sound:
- calm
- short
- honest
- strategy-aware
- non-directive

## Drift to block
- “act now”
- countdown energy
- false certainty
- return-to-app guilt prompts
- background behavior that violates trust rules
