# Support

## What this means
A price area where buying tends to appear.

## Why it matters
It helps explain why some declines slow, pause, or bounce.

## How to think about it
Treat support as a zone that can strengthen or fail, not as a perfect line.

## Common misunderstanding
Assuming support will always hold.
