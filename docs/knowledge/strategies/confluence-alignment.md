# Confluence Alignment

## What this strategy is for
Confluence Alignment is for situations where different kinds of evidence begin pointing to the same area or idea.

## What it looks for
It starts with [confluence](../glossary/confluence.md), meaning layered agreement rather than repeated copies of the same signal.

## What improves fit
Fit improves when the agreement is independent and strong enough to support [meaningful change](../glossary/meaningful-change.md) in interpretation. As the market proves the read more clearly, [confirmed confidence](../glossary/confirmed-confidence.md) can improve.

## What weakens fit
Fit weakens when the “confluence” is really just clutter or when the evidence stack starts breaking apart under stress.

## What PocketPilot would emphasize
Confluence is disciplined agreement, not magical certainty.

## What a user should learn
More indicators do not automatically mean more truth.
