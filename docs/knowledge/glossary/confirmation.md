# confirmation

## Plain-English meaning
Confirmation means the market is giving stronger evidence that an earlier read is holding up.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, confirmation refers to the build-up of evidence that makes an interpretation more trustworthy than it was earlier. It is stronger than a hopeful signal and weaker than certainty.

## Why it matters
PocketPilot uses confirmation to show progression without pretending the market becomes perfect once it looks better.
