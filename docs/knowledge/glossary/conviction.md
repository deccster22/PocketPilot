# conviction

## Plain-English meaning
Conviction means how strongly you believe a setup or read deserves trust.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, conviction should come from evidence quality, context, and support, not just emotion or urgency.

## Why it matters
PocketPilot should strengthen conviction carefully, not cosmetically.
