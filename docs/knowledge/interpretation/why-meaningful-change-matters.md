# Why Meaningful Change Matters

## What this means
[Meaningful change](../glossary/meaningful-change.md) is change that actually affects interpretation. It is more than motion. It is motion that matters.

## Why it matters
PocketPilot is not trying to react to every twitch. It is trying to highlight shifts that should actually change how the market is read, how strategies feel, or what deserves attention.

## What PocketPilot is trying to help you see
This concept helps PocketPilot stay calm. If the market is moving but not changing in a way that matters, the product should avoid overreacting. If the market is changing in a way that does matter, the product should help users notice that clearly.

## Common mistake
A common mistake is mistaking activity for importance.
