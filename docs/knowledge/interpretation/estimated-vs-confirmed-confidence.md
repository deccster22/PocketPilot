# Estimated vs Confirmed Confidence

## What this means
[Estimated confidence](../glossary/estimated-confidence.md) means the evidence is leaning in a direction, but the picture is not fully settled. [Confirmed confidence](../glossary/confirmed-confidence.md) means the evidence has become stronger and more durable than it was earlier.

## Why it matters
This distinction helps PocketPilot stay honest.

## How PocketPilot uses it
PocketPilot uses estimated and confirmed confidence to show progression rather than pretending the market jumps from uncertainty straight into certainty.

## Common mistake
A common mistake is treating early improvement like full confirmation, or treating stronger confirmation like a guarantee.
