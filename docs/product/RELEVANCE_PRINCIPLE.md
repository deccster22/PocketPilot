# RELEVANCE_PRINCIPLE.md

## Purpose
Defines the filtering law behind what PocketPilot shows, suppresses, highlights, or ignores.

## Founding stance
PocketPilot does not win by showing more. It wins by showing what matters.

The canonical order stays:
**Strategy → Profile → Relevance**

That means:
- strategy decides what matters
- profile decides how it is surfaced
- relevance decides whether it deserves attention at all

## Product job
Relevance protects PocketPilot from:
- signal spam
- dashboard bloat
- alert clutter
- educational overgrowth
- “more data = better product” nonsense

## Operating rule
Something may be true without being relevant.
Something may be interesting without being relevant.
Something may even be important in the market and still not deserve surface space for this user, on this account, on this screen.

## Drift to block
- indicator stacking
- alert volume inflation
- clutter masquerading as sophistication
- beginner overload
- advanced-user punishment by density
