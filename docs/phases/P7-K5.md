# P7-K5 - Contextual Knowledge Density / Placement Refinement

## Why this phase happened now

P7-K4 proved that the canonical contextual-knowledge lane could live calmly on Dashboard and Trade Hub without becoming a feed, a gate, or a second message system. P7-K5 happened now because the rollout was stable enough to refine how much of that lane appears, how prominent it feels, and when it should quietly collapse back out of view.

## What was refined

- one explicit service-owned contextual-knowledge presentation contract
- one density/placement shaping seam that keeps the lane profile- and surface-aware
- one calmer Dashboard and Trade Hub presentation path that can show less when relevance is thinner
- one minimal app-side visual refinement that renders the prepared presentation instead of inventing its own density rules

## What was deliberately not added

- no push notifications
- no background polling
- no forced tutorials
- no execution lockouts
- no gating based on knowledge completion
- no giant recommendation feed
- no app-owned topic selection logic
- no Snapshot expansion
- no inbox, badge, or campaign mechanics

## What future P7 work can build on top of it

- additional contextual surfaces only when they have honest relevance
- slightly richer presentation shaping if a later rung needs it
- more follow-through into topic detail without moving selection into `app/`
- future density tuning that stays subordinate to the current surface and does not become a feed

## Recommendation handling

- Adopt now: explicit density and placement shaping, plus the calmer surface refinement
- Add to backlog: future surface-specific presentation tuning and any broader contextual expansion
- Decline for now: push, inbox, badge mechanics, background monitoring, gating, or a recommendation feed
