# volatility

## Plain-English meaning
Volatility means price is moving around more sharply, quickly, or unpredictably than usual.

## Canonical definition
In PocketPilot, volatility refers to the amount and character of market movement. It can mean bigger swings, faster changes, or a more unstable environment. Volatility is not automatically good or bad. It is context.

## Why it matters
PocketPilot treats volatility as something that changes strategy comfort, confidence, and explanation tone. It is not just a drama meter.
